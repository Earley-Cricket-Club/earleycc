Earley Cricket Club

We are Earley Cricket Club in Reading started in 2007. The club is run by passionate volunteers, we have 40+ members from different age groups, ethnicity, and backgrounds. Our cricket club is not just a sports facility; it's a haven for camaraderie, a breeding ground for talent - some of the kids who started their journey here are playing county level cricket now, and a place where memories are made.

Earley CC on Play-Cricket

Our Teams:

  • 2x Saturday League Teams (TVL Division 6B and Division 8A)
  • 1x Sunday friendly
  • 2x Midweek team
  • 1x Indoor team
  • 1x Women's Softball Team

Cricket Nets:

The club is proud to have two cricket nets available for training which takes place on Thursday evenings from 6pm. If you would like to join us at nets then please get in touch via email.

Help Support Us:

Our club is run by the funds raised through memberships and fundraising events. In the last few years, we invested the money raised on Cricket pitch, nets, lawn mover, roller, pitch covers, sheds, pavilion etc but as we look to grow the club we need your support.

Earley Cricket Club is a registered Community Amateur Sports Club (CASC) which means your donations can have tax benefits as well as helping the club and the community. To find out how you can support us please get in touch.
