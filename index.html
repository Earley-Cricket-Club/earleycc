Earley CC has been in the Berkshire Cricket League for 12 years.
The club currently runs 2 Saturday teams: the 1st XI plays in Division 1 and the 2nd XI in Division 2.
We also have a midweek team which plays league and cup T20s.
Starting in 2024 we have a Women's softball team.

Sponsorship opportunities are available, please contact us to find out more.
